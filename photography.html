Fort Jesus

Fort Jesus, built by the Portuguese in 1593. Is a world heritage site. It was designed to protect the port of Mombasa and has seen centuries of conflict and cultural exchange.

Hell's Kitchen

Hell's Kitchen in marafa, also known as Nyari, is a sandstone canyon formed by centuries of erosion. Its layered colors and rugged terrain have earned it the name "Kenya's Grand Canyon."
